The Problem

WhatsApp groups weren't built for notes

Every student knows the pain. Notes get buried, subjects get mixed, and finding anything takes forever.

Scroll through 5,000 images

Notes get buried under memes, voice notes and random chats. Finding one topic means scrolling forever.

No organisation by subject

Physics notes, Math assignments, History summaries — all mixed in one endless chat with no way to filter.

Can't request specific notes

You need Chapter 5 notes but don't know who has them. There's no way to ask the group without spamming.

↓ Until now.
Features

Everything your study group needs

Purpose-built for sharing, organising, and finding notes — fast.

Group Chat

Real-time messaging with your study group, just like you're used to.

Request Notes

Tap once to notify your whole group that you need notes for a specific subject and topic.

Send Tagged Notes

Pick images, tag with subject and topic, arrange page order, and send — all in one flow.

Notes Manager

Every note ever shared, organised by subject and topic. Find anything in seconds, not hours.

Friends & Groups

Search by username, send friend requests, and create study groups with your classmates.

Secure Login

Username and email based login. Your notes and chats are private to your group.

The Flagship Feature

Never lose a note again.

Every note shared in any group is stored in the Notes Manager — tagged, searchable, and organised forever.

  • Filter by subject — see only Physics, only Maths, only History
  • Filter by topic — find Chapter 5 notes instantly
  • See who sent each note and when
  • Notes stay organised even as the chat grows

How It Works

Simple as 1, 2, 3

No learning curve. If you've used WhatsApp, you already know how to use StudySync.

1

Join or create a group

Sign up with your username, add friends, and create a study group with your classmates.

2

Request or send notes

Tap to request notes for any subject and topic, or respond to a request by sending tagged images.

3

Find notes instantly

Open Notes Manager, filter by subject or topic, and find exactly what you need in seconds.
